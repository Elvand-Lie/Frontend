About The Trainer

Where ancient wisdom meets modern business strategy.

Jose He (何德伟)

LEAD TRAINER & LIFE COACH

Coming from a family with deep expertise in geomancy, Jose has always been connected to ancient wisdom and practices. However, the path wasn't always clear. It was after experiencing a profound moment of awakening (大运, 命运觉醒) that he realized his true purpose.

He understood that his destiny was not just to inherit, but to elevate his family's legacy—to 发扬光大 (bring it to new heights). This revelation led him to embrace his true Life Path (轨道).

Today, Jose dedicates his life to helping others achieve clarity, success, and transformation. He bridges the gap between ancient metaphysical wisdom and modern business strategies, standing as a leader, speaker, and coach who empowers individuals and organizations to navigate life with purpose.

Jose has worked with high-level professionals across Southeast Asia, including in Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia.
